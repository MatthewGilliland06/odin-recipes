Mozzarella Sticks

Ingredients:

  • 12 mozzarella string cheese sticks
  • 2 cups Italian-style breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • Marinara sauce, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the cheese:
    1. Unwrap the mozzarella string cheese sticks and cut each one in half to make 24 shorter sticks.
    2. Place the cheese sticks in the freezer for about 30 minutes to firm up. This helps prevent them from melting too quickly during frying.
  2. Set up the breading stations:
    1. In one shallow dish, place the flour.
    2. In a second shallow dish, beat the eggs and milk together.
    3. In a third shallow dish, combine the breadcrumbs, garlic powder, onion powder, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Bread the cheese sticks:
    1. Roll each cheese stick in the flour, shaking off any excess.
    2. Dip each floured cheese stick into the egg mixture, coating it completely.
    3. Roll the cheese stick in the breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to adhere.
    4. Repeat the egg and breadcrumb coating for a double layer of breading (this helps to keep the cheese from leaking out during frying).
    5. Place the breaded cheese sticks on a baking sheet and freeze them for at least 1 hour before frying.
  4. Fry the cheese sticks:
    1. Heat about 2 inches of vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large heavy-bottomed pot to 350°F (175°C).
    2. Fry the mozzarella sticks in batches, being careful not to overcrowd the pot, for about 1-2 minutes or until golden brown and crispy.
    3. Remove the fried mozzarella sticks with a slotted spoon and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
  5. Serve:
    1. Serve the hot mozzarella sticks immediately with marinara sauce for dipping.

Enjoy your delicious homemade mozzarella sticks!
