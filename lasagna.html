Classic Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • For the meat sauce:
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    • 1 pound ground beef
    • 1 pound Italian sausage, casing removed
    • 1 small onion, finely chopped
    • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
    • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
    • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
    • 1/2 cup water
    • 2 tablespoons sugar
    • 1 teaspoon dried basil
    • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
    • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • For the cheese mixture:
    • 1 container (15 ounces) ricotta cheese
    • 1 egg, beaten
    • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • For assembly:
    • 12 lasagna noodles
    • 1 pound mozzarella cheese, shredded
    • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the meat sauce:
    1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
    2. Add the ground beef, Italian sausage, and chopped onion. Cook until the meat is browned and the onion is translucent, about 10 minutes.
    3. Add the minced garlic and cook for another 1-2 minutes.
    4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water.
    5. Add the sugar, basil, Italian seasoning, salt, pepper, and parsley. Stir well to combine.
    6. Bring the sauce to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low. Let it simmer uncovered for 30-45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  2. Prepare the cheese mixture:
    1. In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, beaten egg, parsley, and salt. Mix well and set aside.
  3. Cook the lasagna noodles:
    1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente.
    2. Drain the noodles and rinse them with cold water to prevent sticking. Lay them out on a clean towel or baking sheet.
  4. Assemble the lasagna:
    1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
    3. Place 3-4 lasagna noodles over the sauce, overlapping slightly if necessary.
    4. Spread one-third of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
    5. Sprinkle one-third of the shredded mozzarella cheese over the ricotta layer.
    6. Spoon one-third of the meat sauce over the mozzarella layer.
    7. Repeat these layers twice more, ending with a layer of meat sauce.
    8. Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese evenly over the top.
  5. Bake the lasagna:
    1. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil. To prevent sticking, you can spray the foil with cooking spray or ensure it does not touch the cheese.
    2. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
    3. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the lasagna is bubbly and the cheese is melted and slightly browned.
    4. Let the lasagna cool for about 15 minutes before serving to allow it to set.

Enjoy your classic homemade lasagna!
