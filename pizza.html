Classic Homemade Pizza

Ingredients:

  • For the dough:
    • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast (1 packet)
    • 1 1/2 cups warm water (110째F or 45째C)
    • 3 1/2 to 4 cups all-purpose flour
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 2 teaspoons sugar
    • 2 teaspoons salt
  • For the sauce:
    • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
    • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • 1 teaspoon dried basil
    • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For the toppings:
    • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    • Desired toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives, etc.)
    • Fresh basil leaves (optional, for garnish)
    • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional, for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the dough:
    1. In a large bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.
    2. Add the olive oil, sugar, and salt to the yeast mixture.
    3. Gradually add the flour, one cup at a time, until the dough comes together and is no longer sticky.
    4. Turn the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
    5. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-1.5 hours, or until it has doubled in size.
  2. Prepare the sauce:
    1. In a medium bowl, combine the tomato sauce, tomato paste, oregano, basil, garlic powder, onion powder, sugar, salt, and pepper.
    2. Mix well until all the ingredients are thoroughly combined. Set aside.
  3. Assemble the pizza:
    1. Preheat your oven to 475째F (245째C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to preheat as well.
    2. Punch down the risen dough and divide it into two equal parts for two pizzas.
    3. On a lightly floured surface, roll out each portion of dough into a 12-inch circle.
    4. Transfer the rolled-out dough onto a piece of parchment paper or a pizza peel if using a pizza stone.
    5. Spread a generous amount of sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
    6. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
    7. Add your desired toppings over the cheese.
  4. Bake the pizza:
    1. Carefully transfer the assembled pizza onto the preheated pizza stone or a baking sheet.
    2. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
    3. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.
  5. Garnish and serve:
    1. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
    2. Slice and serve hot.

Enjoy your homemade pizza!
